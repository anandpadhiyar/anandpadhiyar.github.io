Me actually samaj raha tha ki Riya ye mere liye care kar rahi hai. mene Riya se dil se mafi mangi aur uske baad usne muje ek achchi khabar bhi sunayi. Guess karo kya hoga vo...

Papa ne pura pucha ki kisiko kuch hua nahi na. Riya theek hai na. To actually me bohot khush tha ki yeeeee.....Riya bhi abhi thodi khush thi ki papa se baat start ho gai.
